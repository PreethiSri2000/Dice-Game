Game Instructions

  1. Two Players can play the game. First choose the player.
  2. Set your target score. Without target score you can't play the game.
  3. After setting the target score, start to roll the dice.
  4. Player 1 will always starts the game.
  5. If you want to add the current score to your Score, press hold.
  6. Once you pressed the Hold, the player's turn will be switched.
  7. If "1" occured in the dice, player's turn will be switched.
  8. The player who reached the target score or more than that first, will win the game.
